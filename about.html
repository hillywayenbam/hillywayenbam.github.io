Origin story

She did not find writing.
Writing found her first.

Hilly grew up in Manipur, a corner of India so layered with folklore, colour, and quiet defiance that storytelling was never a hobby. It was the air. She breathed it in and never stopped exhaling worlds.

The stories came first. Fantasy novels with goddesses who burn. Campus romances that flip every tired script. Characters so real they argue back during edits. But somewhere between one story and the next, Hilly noticed something. The same instinct that builds a fictional world also builds a brand. The same craft that makes a reader turn a page at midnight makes a client click a call to action at noon.

Every great story knows exactly where it wants you to end up. So does every great content strategy.

So she became both. An author who treats her fiction like a universe worth protecting. A content strategist who treats your brand like a story worth telling. She brings the same obsessive attention to the arc of a chapter and the structure of a content calendar, because to her, they are the same discipline wearing different clothes.

Several years across health and wellness, lifestyle, culture, and travel writing. Brand voices shaped. Editorial strategies built. Content teams held to the highest standards. And somehow, through all of it, she still found time to fall completely in love with her own characters. That is not a contradiction. That is just Hilly.

The Author

She writes fantasy novels with goddesses who discover themselves in the middle of impossible political arrangements. She writes campus romances where love surprises everyone, including the characters. She writes coming of age stories for people who grew up with nothing but the stubborn belief that something better was waiting. And she writes a non fiction guide that reads like a friend holding your hand through the scariest week of your life. Her fiction is not an escape from real life. It is a braver version of it.

The Strategist

She has spent years inside the engine room of content. Writing, editing, running quality checks, shaping the kind of brand voice that makes readers feel genuinely seen. She understands SEO without becoming robotic about it. She understands your audience before you finish explaining who they are. She does not just produce content. She architects the reason your audience comes back, and keeps coming back, and eventually tells everyone they know.

How she thinks

01

Every word earns its place.

She writes like every sentence is paying rent. If it is not doing something, it does not stay. Not in her novels. Not in your blog post. Not anywhere on the page.

02

The reader is always in the room.

She writes with the reader sitting right across from her. She knows what makes them lean in. She knows the exact moment they would click away, and she makes absolutely sure that moment never comes.

03

Strategy without soul is just noise.

She has seen content plans that look brilliant on a spreadsheet and fall completely flat on the page. She refuses to make that trade. Every strategy she builds has a heartbeat in it.
