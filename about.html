About

The person behind the prose

A writer who believes
stories move people.

More than words —
a way of seeing.

Writing found Hilly before she found it. Growing up in Manipur, she was always the one reaching for stories — in the northeast's layered culture, in the quietness of everyday rituals, in the way a place can hold so much feeling without saying a word.

Over the past several years, that instinct has taken shape as a career. She has written and edited content across health & wellness, lifestyle, and travel — not because these are tidy categories, but because they all circle back to the same thing: how people live, and what makes that life feel worth living.

Her work has appeared across digital publications and brand platforms, where she has shaped content strategies, refined brand voices, and brought editorial rigour to fast-moving content teams.

What drives her is not output but resonance. A piece that stays with the reader. A headline that captures exactly the feeling a brand has been trying to put into words for months.

Writing philosophy

01

Stories first, always.

Every brief — no matter how technical or commercial — has a human being at the other end of it. Hilly writes to reach that person, not just to rank for a keyword.

02

Clarity is a kindness.

Good writing does not perform intelligence. It makes complex ideas feel natural and obvious to the reader — which is harder than it looks.

03

Voice is the brand.

Brands with a genuine voice are remembered. Hilly's editing work is built on this — sharpening and protecting a brand's tone as fiercely as its ideas.

04

Details are everything.

It is the specific detail — the right statistic, the unexpected anecdote, the word that lands just so — that turns content into something people want to share.
