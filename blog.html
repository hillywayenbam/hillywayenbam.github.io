Thoughts

Straight from my head

I write here
when I cannot not.

Not a content strategy. Not a personal brand play. Just me, thinking out loud about writing, storytelling, life in Manipur, and everything else that refuses to stay quiet.

I have been told I should have a content strategy for my own blog. A niche. A posting schedule. A target keyword.

I respectfully declined. This space is mine.

Here you will find whatever I was thinking about when the thought got too big to keep inside. Some posts will be about craft. Some will be about the chaos of writing a fantasy novel while also writing someone else's content calendar. Some will be about my cat. Some will be about growing up in a place that does not always show up on the map but has always shown up in my writing.

There are no categories here. There is just me. If that is the kind of writing you like to read, welcome. Pull up a chair. I am glad you found this corner.
